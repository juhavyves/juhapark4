The Black Rabbit and The Table of Abundance Editorial 2023
A calendar celebrates 2023, the year of the black rabbit and abundance. It was designed by comparing the abundance of 2023 to the rich dinner table of a family. Like this rich table, it is a calendar containing the hope that it will be a year to build abundance one by one every month.
